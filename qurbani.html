Qurbani program

Organized giving with care, planning, and dignity.

The Qurbani program is not treated as a side campaign. It uses Bloodman’s volunteer network and logistics discipline to handle collection and distribution responsibly.

Program promise

Transparent collection and responsible distribution.

Bloodman organizes contributions and local delivery so support reaches under-served families through a process that stays visible and accountable.

Why it fits Bloodman

The same network, applied seasonally.

Qurbani works because Bloodman already has the volunteer structure, communication habits, and public trust needed to execute it carefully.

1

Confirm participation

Interested contributors reach out to the Bloodman team to coordinate contribution and planning.

2

Prepare local routes

Volunteers identify priority areas and plan distributions that are practical and respectful.

3

Deliver with accountability

The program is executed as organized service work, not improvised charity.
