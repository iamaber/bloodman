News & Events

What we've been doing.

Current

Blood donation response stays central

Bloodman continues to foreground urgent blood support while widening access to telemedicine, outreach, and community relief programs. The coordination hotline remains active 24 hours a day, every day of the year.

Featured

World Bloodman Day remains a flagship touchpoint

Public celebration and awareness continue to matter because they turn a behind-the-scenes support network into an organization people can recognize and join. The annual event draws volunteers, families, and media coverage nationwide.

Ongoing

Blood, care, and relief stay connected

The organization's most important evolution is structural: blood support, telemedicine, camps, and relief now read as one coordinated system rather than separate efforts. This integration makes Bloodman more trusted over time.
